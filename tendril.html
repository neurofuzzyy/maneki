Tendril
Tattoo

CollaboratorMina Lim DeliverablesBrand system, 100-page publication, packaging, clothing line, exhibition ToolsIllustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Procreate, Figma

Tendril is a conceptual tattoo studio specializing in custom sigils — symbolic designs created in collaboration with each client. Each sigil encodes personal mantras, memories, or affirmations, resulting in tattoos that are intimate and deeply individual.

Visually, the designs draw from organic patterns found in nature, evoking resilience, memory, and transformation. The final project was exhibited at the UW 2025 Senior Design Show, where we also sold a screen-printed clothing line and gave temporary tattoos.
