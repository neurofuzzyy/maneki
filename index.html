About
Design that works
as hard as you do.

Most small businesses deserve a better website than they have. I'm here to fix that — without the agency price tag.

I'm Emily, a Seattle-based designer with a degree from the University of Washington and a background in brand identity, web design, and visual systems. I started Maneki Studio because I kept seeing local businesses with outdated sites, no sites, or sites that looked like everyone else's. You've put years into your business. Your website should show it.

Every project I take on is built from scratch around your business — not dropped into a template. I handle the design, the build, and the launch. You stay focused on what you actually do.
