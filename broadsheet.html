1 in 292 Million

In collaboration with Mina Lim Information is Beautiful Awards Shortlist · Nightingale

An in-depth exploration of lottery systems in America, examining the structure and impact of the Powerball lottery. Through data analysis and visualization, the project investigates the financial and social effects of the lottery and its influence on different communities.

The project spans a 5-page broadsheet, an interactive mobile microsite, and a guerrilla sticker campaign — each format reframing the same statistics to make the odds feel as impossible as they actually are.

We also designed a sticker campaign — a simple, visual way to put the odds in front of people right where they buy tickets.
