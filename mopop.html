Symbol Set

ClientMuseum of Pop Culture DeliverablesSymbol set, poster, billboard, merchandise

A custom symbol set for the Museum of Pop Culture in Seattle — bold, graphic marks built to capture the energy of music, film, gaming, and pop culture at every scale.

Each symbol works from a favicon to a full-face billboard. The system draws from MoPOP's eclectic identity while maintaining a unified visual language that's immediately ownable.

Delivered as a full symbol system with poster and merchandise applications.
